About Klemson

Klemson is a DJ whose world revolves around UK Garage and Hard Bounce — two territories bound by their relationship to percussive impact and precisely-tuned groove. His selection cuts straight to the point: bass that speaks before words, a tempo that leaves no room to breathe.

For this fourth episode of Phat & Phurious FM, Klemson held his set in the cà phê sữa đá atmosphere, confirming that the format embraces both UK pressure and the most instinctive garage flow.
