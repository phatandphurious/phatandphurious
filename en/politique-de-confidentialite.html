Privacy Policy

Effective June 1, 2026. In accordance with the GDPR (EU Regulation 2016/679)

Data controller

Data collected

This site only collects data that you voluntarily provide via the contact form:

  • Name or alias: to identify your request
  • Email address: to respond to you

No tracking cookies, no browsing data and no location data are collected by this site.

Purpose of processing

Data collected via the contact form is used solely to respond to your request. It is neither resold, nor shared with third parties for commercial purposes, nor used for prospecting.

This site uses the following third-party services, which may process data about you:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com): loads the Poppins font. This involves a request to Google's servers when the page loads. Google Privacy Policy →
  • YouTube (youtube.com): episode thumbnails are loaded from YouTube servers. Video playback takes place on the YouTube platform. YouTube Privacy Policy →

Retention period

Data submitted via the contact form is retained for the time necessary to process your request, then deleted within a maximum period of 12 months.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of the data concerning you
  • Right of rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: have your data deleted
  • Right to object: object to the processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@phatandphurious.com. We will respond within a maximum of 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the CNIL (French data protection authority): www.cnil.fr

Cookies

This site uses no first-party cookies for tracking or analytics purposes. No cookie consent banner is therefore required for our own use.

The third-party services mentioned above (Google Fonts, YouTube) may set their own cookies when loading their resources. We have no control over these cookies.

Modifications

This privacy policy may be updated at any time. The effective date shown at the top of this page is authoritative. We encourage you to consult it regularly.
